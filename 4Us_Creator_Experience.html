Your Commitments

Keep it real.

Your Obligations

  • Connect a verified Stripe account before cycle end
  • Promote events through legitimate channels only
  • Use 4Us tools to verify check-ins at free events
  • Notify us within 48 hrs of any event cancellation
  • Report any suspected fraud or abuse you discover

Not Allowed

  • Fake RSVPs or ticket purchases of any kind
  • Checking in attendees who did not attend
  • Buying tickets to your own events
  • Coordinating to artificially inflate numbers
  • Exporting attendee data outside 4Us

Policies

We've got a fair process.

Disputes

You have 10 business days from receiving your payout report to raise any discrepancy. Our team will work with you directly to reach a fair resolution.

Refunds

If a ticket is refunded post-payout, the $0.50 sponsorship for that ticket is deducted from your next cycle. We'll notify you within 5 business days.

Program terms may be updated with 30 days' written notice. The RSVP Track is a 90-day pilot — reviewed at conclusion.
